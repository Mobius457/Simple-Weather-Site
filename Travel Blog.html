Rocket City to the Rocky Mountains:

A Weather-Fueled Adventure

This past fall, I embarked on an epic road trip through the American West. From the space-age wonders of Huntsville, Alabama, to the majestic peaks of the Colorado Rockies, I experienced a kaleidoscope of landscapes, cultures, and weather. Join me as I recount the highlights of this unforgettable journey.

Day 1: Huntsville, Alabama - Blast Off!

The Space and Rocket Center was even more impressive than I imagined. Standing beneath the massive Saturn V rocket, I felt a sense of awe and wonder. It's incredible to think that this machine launched humans to the moon! The weather was perfect for exploring - clear skies and a comfortable temperature.

Day 2: Nashville, Tennessee - Music City Vibes

After a hearty breakfast of biscuits and gravy, I hit the road for Nashville. The drive was scenic, with rolling hills and vibrant fall foliage. As I got closer to the city, the air crackled with the energy of live music. I caught an amazing bluegrass show at The Bluebird Cafe and indulged in some delicious hot chicken. The weather was a bit cooler here, but the warm hospitality more than made up for it.
